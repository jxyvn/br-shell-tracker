About

Buckshot Roulette Shell Tracker is a tool for keeping a consistent count of the shells left in the rounds rotation.

Using this tool allows players to make better judgements with their turn, and hopefully last longer.

While we make your game much easier, this doesn't guarantee your spot as the winner.

Remember, this is more of a tool to calculate your odds of having a red shell in the chamber, rather than your chances at winning.
