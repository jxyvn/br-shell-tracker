How to Use

After launching Buckshot Roulette, open up your browser and visit our home page.

Resize your browser window on either the top left or right side of your game.

If you have the "Always on Top" feature of your browser, use that so the tracker doesn't disappear when you click in-game.

If your browser doesn't support "Always on Top", then feel free to use our service on your mobile phone.

If you have more than one monitor, ignore both of those options if you choose to.
